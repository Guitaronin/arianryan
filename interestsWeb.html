Web Design

This is a relatively new hobby for me. Eighteen months ago I didn't know anything about computers. Then I moved to L.A. to pursue music, and started taking temp office jobs to get a foothold. Eventually one of these was for a large internet company (maybe only medium sized compared to Google, Amazon, or EBay). Through my dedication this job grew into a full time position in the marketing department. Slowly my role became more and more technical. As I was increasingly exposed to HTML, I became increasingly interested in learning it. I then began taking online tutorials for HTML and CSS. Then I got a book called Integrated HTML and CSS. Finally I began learning Photoshop, and the end result is this website. For the record this site was completely hand coded by yours truly, without the extensive aid of any outside resources.

I'm still very interested in increasing my knowledge of web design as well as learning some programming languages. As I make new discoveries, I'll publish some information in this section.
