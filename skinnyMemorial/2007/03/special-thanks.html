Skinny Afterthoughts

Wednesday, March 21, 2007

Special Thanks

Although Skinny was elderly in terms of cat lifespans, her vitality was strong and her passing felt very sudden. This made coping with the loss even more difficult. Fortunately Skinny was cared for in her final hours by a wonderful, caring veterinary clinic, Center Sinai, in Culver City, CA. We would like to extend our deepest gratitude to Center Sinai for their consistency in providing high quality health care to our animal friends. And also, for their warmth, compassion, and sympathy in this difficult experience.

We would also like to thank Cal Pet for their services, and comforting customer support.

Center Sinai and Cal Pet, thank you very much.
