Freelance Musician

I played my first gig at age 16. Over the past 15 years I have performed as a regular member of 8 bands. However, the bulk of my professional experience has come from working as a freelance guitarist and bassist. I have filled in with bands when regular members were unavailable, been a part of or organized "one time" engagements with other freelancers, worked as a session guitarist on recording projects, and performed "reading" gigs.

My approach is sensitive, creative, and versatile. I have performed nearly all popular styles of music, and have an extensive background in free improvisation. Additionally, I always endeavor to be professional - show up on time, dressed appropriately, with pro gear (and backup gear), and no ego.

I have the following skills to offer:

  • Excellent at accompanying singers and soloists
  • Strong lead playing in jazz, blues, and rock
  • Solid with funk and Latin rhythms
  • Very comfortable in the studio
  • Reading - cold reading is a bit of a weakness, but I can generally nail stuff down within 5 - 10 minutes of receiving the music
  • Willing to travel

If any of this sounds like it may fulfill a need for you, send me a message with your contact info.

Looking forward to hearing from you,

Arian
