Skinny Afterthoughts

Sunday, March 18, 2007

Introduction

In developing this online memorial to our friend Skinny, we realized that over time we would continue to remember anecdotes about our time with her that we failed to include in the site originally. We hope to preserve every memory that comes to us with this blog.

Also, we welcome any memories and/or thoughts that others would like to share.

Rest In Peace Skinny, we miss you.
