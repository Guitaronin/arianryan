Family Background:

I like to consider myself a true example of what America is all about - a cultural melting pot. For the most part the details are not completely clear to me. I do know my bloodline contains significant parts of Native American (Blackfoot and Cherokee), and Irish. My grandfather came to this country from Czechoslovakia. There are also (I believe) contributions from German, Welsh, and French immigrants.
