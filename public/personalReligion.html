Religion

Live and let live.

I'm interested in spirituality and therefore tend to be interested in the study of religion. I guess you could say that I'm a believer in the Tao and the teachings of Lao Tsu, and also the practice of Zen Buddhism. However, I don't see these in the same way that most westerners would define religion. Additionally, I'm probably the worst student of both of those studies of all time. Regardless of the religion with which one identifies them self, there are a few ultimate truths that humanity is trying to observe.
