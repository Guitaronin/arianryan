Online Video Rental

Blockbuster vs. Netflix

I love to watch movies. I have always been a frequent movie renter. As far as I'm concerned there has never been a better option for people who like to rent movies than there is right now with online video rental. It's fast. It's easy. And, it saves you money!

How it works:

  1. You create a list of movies that you want to see
  2. They send you the first three movies on your list, and you receive them in the mail the next day
  3. You keep them as long as you want - no late fees, ever!
  4. When you're ready to send a movie back, you put it in the provided envelope and drop it in the mailbox
  5. When they receive the movie, they send you the next one on your list

Don't waste another minute driving to the store, and don't waste another dollar on late fees!

Service Provider

Extra Features In-store exchange Free movie downloads

These last two features are both awesome, and probably deserve some further elaboration.

  • Blockbuster allows you to exchange your movies at any one of their stores, so that you don't have to wait for the mailing process.
  • Netflix allows you to download streaming movies - anything in thier catalog - in addition to the DVDs you receive as a part of your standard plan.
